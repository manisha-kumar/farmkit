About Us

FarmKit is a Market Place for farmers and all other agriculture stake holders. Farmers can sell their crops, natural manures, cattle, Dairy used farm machinery etc using this e-Trading Platform. Farmers can also transparently buy inputs and implements using online shopping and ecommerce platform. Farmers (Kisan) can sell their produce directly to end consumer i.e. Institution , Group Co-operative Societies , Citizen Associations or any other group buyers & also farmer can save lot of money via Group buying of Agri products or machinery directly from Manufacturing Companies and big distributors on a fair price.

Who Are We

Farmkit help our consumers to consume the fresh farm products directly from the farm with affordable rates which include products like rice, wheat, jowar, cereal, fruits, vegetables, milk, ghee, eggs, etc.

What We Do

Farmkit is a place for the farmers to sell their crops,cattles,diary,fruits,vegetables,etc using e-trading platform.Farmers can implement their outputs using online shopping and ecommerce platform.Farmkit ensures to provide best customer services to its consumers.It provides all the available products less than one days so that the consumer consume fresh products.
